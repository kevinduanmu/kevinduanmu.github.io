About Me

Welcome! I am a Professor at the Institute for Advanced Study in Mathematics, Harbin Institute of Technology. My research interests include nonstandard analysis, mathematical economics, general equilibrium theory, Markov processes, and statistical decision theory. I received my Ph.D. degree in Statistics from University of Toronto in 2018.
